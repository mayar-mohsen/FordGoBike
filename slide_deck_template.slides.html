Investigation Overview¶

In this presentation, we will focus on the member genders (Males, Females, Others) and user type (Customer, Subscriber) and see the difference between them in member birth year and trips duration

Dataset Overview¶

This data set includes information about individual rides made in a bike-sharing system covering the greater San Francisco Bay area

Note:¶

In the following presentation, the member birth year will be starting from 1940 and the duration will end at 3000, to remove the outliers so we can have a better look on the data

  • Males have the greatest number then females then others

Member gender VS. Duration¶

  • Males have the shortest duration trips
  • Females have a long duration trips "longer than males but shorter than others"
  • Others have the longest duration trips
